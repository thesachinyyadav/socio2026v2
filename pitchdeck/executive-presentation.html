Current Institutional Challenges

When activities run through separate chats, spreadsheets, and manual follow-ups, institutions lose speed, visibility, and accountability.

Approval Bottlenecks

Event go-live often depends on scattered communication across departments, creating delays and uncertainty.

Fragmented Communication

Students, organisers, and offices receive updates through different channels, causing confusion and missed actions.

Operational Disconnect

Venue, catering, stalls, and volunteer coordination are often disconnected from event planning timelines.

Manual Attendance Burden

Physical or ad-hoc attendance handling increases errors and reduces trust in post-event reporting.

Limited Leadership Visibility

Senior decision makers struggle to get one real-time picture of participation, outcomes, and trends.

Poor Audit Readiness

Without centralized records, proving compliance and governance quality becomes difficult during reviews.

Feature by Feature Breakdown

Each module is designed around practical campus workflows and measurable institutional value.

1. Event Discovery and Smart Registration

Students explore upcoming events and fests, then register individually or in teams with event-specific forms.

Problem solved: low participation visibility
Who benefits: students, organisers
Discover Experience
Before

Students search across scattered notices and miss deadlines.

After

One discovery hub with direct registration and confirmation flow.

Workflow
  1. Student opens Discover and filters events/fests.
  2. Student reviews event details, rules, timing, and eligibility.
  3. Student submits individual or team registration.
  4. SOCIO creates digital ticket with QR access.
  5. Student receives centralized confirmation and reminders.

2. Guided Event and Fest Creation

Organisers can create complete event plans with custom participant fields, fees, deadlines, and operational details.

Problem solved: inconsistent planning quality
Who benefits: organisers, review offices
Before

Important details are missed and clarified repeatedly.

After

Standardized capture improves readiness and reduces rework.

Workflow
  1. Organiser fills a structured event or fest form.
  2. Uploads banners/brochures and sets registration rules.
  3. Saves as draft or submits for institutional review.
  4. Approval status is tracked in one place.
  5. After clearance, event becomes available publicly.

3. Stage-Based Approval Workflow

SOCIO supports sequential institutional approvals and stage tracking with accountability history.

Problem solved: approval ambiguity
Who benefits: HOD, Dean, CFO, Accounts, organisers
Before

No single source of truth for who approved what and when.

After

Clear queue, action trail, and stage-wise accountability.

Workflow
  1. Organiser submits event/fest for approval.
  2. System queues item for the relevant role.
  3. Approver reviews, approves, or returns with notes.
  4. Next role receives item after prior stage completion.
  5. Go-live status updates when required stages are complete.

4. QR-Based Attendance and Participant Control

Attendance can be marked via QR scanning and participant-level tracking, improving post-event reporting accuracy.

Problem solved: attendance disputes
Who benefits: organisers, academic reviewers
Before

Manual sheets and post-event reconciliation delays.

After

Faster scan-based tracking with event-level participant records.

Workflow
  1. Participant presents digital QR ticket.
  2. Volunteer/organiser scans ticket at entry.
  3. Status is updated in the attendance record.
  4. Dashboard reflects attendance progress live.
  5. Exportable reports support institutional documentation.

5. Operations Booking: Venue, Catering, Stalls

Execution support is connected directly to event workflows, reducing follow-up effort across offices.

Problem solved: disconnected operations
Who benefits: organisers, operations teams
Before

Separate requests lead to delays and overlap conflicts.

After

Single booking queue with role-based action and status visibility.

Workflow
  1. Organiser submits venue/catering/stall request.
  2. Relevant operational manager receives queue entry.
  3. Request is approved, declined, or sent for revision.
  4. Status syncs back to organiser workflow view.
  5. Execution readiness improves before event day.

6. Notifications and Student Communication

The platform provides centralized updates for registrations, reminders, approvals, and announcements.

Problem solved: missed updates
Who benefits: all stakeholders
Before

Users miss critical updates across fragmented channels.

After

Action-focused notification feed with read-state management.

Workflow
  1. System triggers update based on workflow events.
  2. User sees personal or institutional notification.
  3. User opens destination page from notification link.
  4. Notification status is updated as read/completed.
  5. Broadcasts keep campus community aligned.
