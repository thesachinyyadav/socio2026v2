Gated Access Control

Visitor Management — Controlled Entry for External Guests

External guests and industry visitors do not bypass the system — they flow through it with pre-approval, digital passes, and a live security roster that is always current.

The Gated Connection Principle

Every external person who enters campus for an event is a registered, pre-vetted, time-bound participant — not a walk-in. SOCIO's gated connection layer gives Security a live head-count, gives Admins a complete visitor log, and gives visitors a frictionless, phone-first experience.

Invite Issued Organiser generates a unique, event-specific invite link
Visitor Pre-Registers Name, institution, and ID submitted before arrival
Admin Vets & Approves Request reviewed; approved or declined with reason
Digital Pass Issued Time-bound QR pass delivered to guest's phone or email
Gate Scan & Entry Security scans pass; entry logged with timestamp

Pre-Approval Gate

No visitor enters without an approved digital pass. The invite link is event-specific and time-locked, preventing misuse after the event window closes.

Live Security Roster

Security personnel see a real-time list of expected versus checked-in visitors. Any gap is immediately visible, enabling instant escalation without paperwork.

Time-Bound Passes

Visitor QR passes auto-expire after the event window. Re-entry after expiry is blocked at the gate with no manual revocation required.

Compliance Audit Log

Every visitor interaction — registration, approval, scan, exit — is time-stamped and logged, creating a complete record available to Admin and leadership on demand.

Visitor Count Dashboard

Leaders and security see live breakdowns — total invited, approved, checked-in, and on-campus — with export for incident reporting or NAAC documentation.

Emergency Readiness

In an emergency, the complete on-campus visitor roster — name, organisation, last-scan point — is available in seconds, enabling a structured evacuation response.
