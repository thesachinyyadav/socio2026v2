Campus Event Chaos

The Problem

Universities conduct hundreds of events annually, yet there is no centralized infrastructure for discovery, registration, attendance, and reporting. The result is operational inefficiency across the campus ecosystem.

Fragmented Communication

Events spread across WhatsApp, Instagram, and notice boards — students miss out on relevant opportunities.

Manual Registrations

Google Forms and spreadsheets create errors, duplicates, and operational chaos at scale.

Paper Attendance Tracking

No audit trail, no verification, and inaccurate attendance claims.

Zero Institutional Analytics

Universities lack visibility into engagement, reach, and outcomes.

Administrative Overload

Student councils spend weeks coordinating tasks that should take hours.

No Governance Layer

Roles and access are unmanaged, leading to accountability gaps and security risks.
